About YouTube Commander
YouTube Commander is a power toolkit for playback control, Shorts, playlists, and subscriptions. Everything runs locally unless you enable optional Cloudflare sync.
Player & Playback
  • Seek controls: 3-tier seek buttons + shortcuts with burst indicator.
  • Quality controls: auto-set max quality every playback.
  • Audio track controls: auto-switch to original audio when available.
  • Windowed fullscreen: expand player without native fullscreen.
  • Open in new tab: clean watch URL + channel shortcuts.
  • Video rotation: quick rotate for misoriented videos.
Shorts Enhancements
  • Shorts counter: session indicator near action buttons.
  • Auto-advance: optional auto-scroll to next Short at end.
  • Volume shortcuts: Ctrl+Up/Down adjusts by 5%.
  • Progress bar: keep Shorts progress visible.
  • Upload age: "x ago" labels on Shorts cards.
Subscriptions
  • Subscription Manager: categories, chip filters, quick add, and bulk tools.
  • Context menu: move/add categories directly from cards.
  • Subscribed label: pill on Home feed (handle + channel ID fallback).
  • Hide subscribed videos: optional cleanup toggle for Home feed.
  • CSV import/export: backup category assignments.
Playlists & Multi-Select
  • Multi-select: bulk save/remove from playlists, create playlists.
  • Watch later quick action: one-tap save on the multi-select dock.
  • Mini-guide playlist shortcut: jump to playlists quickly.
History & Sync
  • Watched history: mark watched videos + optional hide watched.
  • Export/import: move history between browsers.
  • Cloudflare sync (optional): watched history + subscriptions to D1.
Navigation
  • Scroll to top: floating quick navigation button.
